Nikola Tesla

Inventor and Electrical Engineer

Here's a time line of Nikola Tesla's life

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Croatia
  • 1875 - Enrolled at the Polytechnic Institute in Graz
  • 1884 - Emigrated to the United States
  • 1891 - Became a naturalized U.S. citizen
  • 1943 - Died in New York City
